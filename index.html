About

The person behind the voice.

I'm Aakanksha. A professional voice artist, dubbing actor, singer, and content creator. I bring scripts to life by blending creative expression with a deep understanding of human behavior.

With an MA in Clinical Psychology and specialized training in Art Therapy, my performances go beyond simply reading lines. I analyze the psychological depth of every character to deliver raw, authentic emotion. Whether it's an intense drama, a commercial campaign, or an insightful film breakdown on my channel Akki TV, my goal is always to make the audience feel.
