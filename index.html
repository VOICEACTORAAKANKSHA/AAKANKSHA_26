About

The person behind the voice.

hello~ i'm Aakanksha a professional voice actor, dubbing artist. I bring scripts to life by blending creative expression with a deep understanding of human behavior.

With an M.A. in Clinical Psychology, my performances go beyond simply reading lines. I analyze the psychological depth of every character to deliver raw, authentic emotion. Whether it's an intense drama, a commercial campaign, or an insightful film breakdown on my channel The Cine Psyche, my goal is always to make the audience feel.
