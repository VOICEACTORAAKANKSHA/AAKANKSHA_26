About

The person behind the voice.

I'm Aakanksha. Voice artist. Singer. Film content creator. Anchor. And someone who spent years studying the human mind — because I believe every great performance starts with understanding people.

With an MA in Clinical Psychology and training in Art Therapy, I bring a depth to my work that goes beyond the script. Whether it's a song cover, a film breakdown, or a voice project — I'm always asking: what does this feel like to a real person?

Because a voice isn't just sound. It's a bridge between a story and someone who needed to hear it.
